About us

Welcome to the BBPS Alumni Portal - a platform designed to keep the Bal Bharati Public School (BBPS) community connected. Whether you're looking to reconnect with old friends, stay informed about alumni events, or network professionally, this portal is your gateway to staying involved with the BBPS family.

Our Mission

This portal aims to strengthen the bond between BBPS alumni by offering a space where graduates can come together, share memories, and remain connected. Whether it's through attending school events or engaging with fellow alumni on social media, we provide a simple way to stay in touch with the school and each other.

What We Offer

• Networking Made Easy: Connect with your fellow alumni through social media links, making it easy to maintain professional and personal connections.
• Events & Reunions: Stay updated on upcoming alumni events, reunions, and other gatherings. Whether in-person or virtual, our events offer opportunities to reconnect and engage with the BBPS community.

Stay Connected

The BBPS Alumni Portal is more than just a website—it's a space where memories are relived, friendships are rekindled, and new connections are made. We invite you to stay involved, attend events, and keep the BBPS spirit alive!
